Serenity Soul senory garden

Join Eucae Bonnets and Serenity Soul to tour our newest garden, the Sensory Garden. Studies have shown that Sensory Gardens provide therapeutic benefits, particularly for children and adults with special needs. This Sensory Garden is devoted to raised plant beds emphasizing the five senses: sight, smell, hearing, taste, and touch.
From the wafting lavender to snappy peas, from rustling grasses to chiming bamboo, this garden tour will inspire your curiosity and green thumb! It’s also designed to permit wheelchair access and other accessibility concerns.
This tour is FREE for children under 12 and is just $5 for general entry. Contact us on how to sign up.

Garden Workshop

Join the Eucae Bonnets for a day of learning about soil, plants, trees, water, and more.
In addition to lectures from our Garden experts – covering lessons learned around the Garden, new trends in horticulture, tropical plants, and planting for wildlife – participants will enjoy opportunities to socialize with presenters and other attendees over coffee, lunch, and a happy hour.
Prices start around $15 and happen twice a month.Contact us on how to sign up.
